Our services

List of Services we Offer to our Patient
Hospital, like many others, provides critical emergency care 24/7. It's equipped to handle various urgent medical situations, including trauma, heart attacks, strokes, severe injuries,
and other life-threatening conditions. The emergency department is staffed with
trained healthcare professionals who prioritize rapid assessment, stabilization, and treatment of patients in need.
Inpatient care involves accommodating patients requiring overnight stays or extended hospitalization for comprehensive treatment.
This includes round-the-clock monitoring, medication administration, therapies, and specialized care tailored to the patient's medical condition or surgical recovery.
The hospital's surgical department is proficient in performing a wide array of surgical procedures, ranging from routine surgeries to complex interventions. Skilled surgeons, anesthesiologists,
and support staff ensure patient safety and optimal
outcomes in various specialties like general surgery, orthopedi
Hospital offers extensive diagnostic facilities including laboratories, imaging centers, and specialized diagnostic procedures. This encompasses blood tests, imaging scans (X-rays, CT scans, MRIs),
ultrasounds, biopsies, and other tests crucial for accurate diagnosis and treatment planning.
The hospital houses various departments catering to specific medical specialties. These encompass cardiology, oncology, neurology,pediatrics, obstetrics andgynecology,psychiatry, endocrinology,
pulmonology, gastroenterology, and more. Each department is staffed with specialized healthcare professionals adept at managing and treating conditions within their field.
ICUs provide highly specialized care for critically ill patients. These units are equipped with advanced monitoring systems and life-supporting equipment, overseen by specialized healthcare teams
to deliver round-the-clock intensive care.
Post-treatment or surgery, patients may require rehabilitation. Hospital offers physical therapy,
occupational therapy, speech therapy, and other rehabilitation services aimed at restoring function, mobility, and improving quality of life.
Comprehensive care for expectant mothers, including prenatal care, labor and delivery, and postnatal care
for both mother and newborns, is provided. Obstetricians, gynecologists, neonatologists, and specialized nursing staff ensure safe childbirth experiences.
The hospital's psychiatric department or mental health services provide evaluation, counseling, therapy, and in some cases, inpatient psychiatric care for individuals facing mental health challenges.
Offers outpatient clinics for consultations, minor procedures, follow-up appointments, and treatments that do not require overnight stays, ensuring ongoing care for various medical conditions.
A pharmacy within the hospital ensures accessibility to prescribed medications for inpatients and outpatients, with pharmacists available to offer
guidance and ensure safe and accurate medication use.
As a teaching hospital, Hospital is involved in medical education and research. It provides training for medical students, residents, and fellows, and conducts research to advance medical knowledge, improve treatments,
and enhance patient care.
